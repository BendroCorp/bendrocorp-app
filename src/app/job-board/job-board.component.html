Job Board Information

The BendroCorp internal job board is intended to provide a way for employees to share jobs internally within BendroCorp. This is not intended to replace the Spectrum job board but augment it with internal types of jobs or jobs with objectives which should remain internal.

All rewards are in operations points (OP) which will be redeemable for UEC at a later time.
